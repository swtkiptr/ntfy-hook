ntfy Hook bridges Android notifications to ntfy servers, enabling:

Phone notifications on computers, tablets, other devices
Selective app notification forwarding
Full history of sent notifications
Real-time connection status indicators
Key Features:

Choose specific apps for notification forwarding
Color-coded real-time status monitoring
Complete log of forwarded notifications
Auto-restart after device reboot
Easy setup with server URL and app selection
ntfy Hook uses Android's notification listener to:

Monitor selected app notifications
Securely forward to ntfy server via HTTPS
Maintain persistent connection
Show real-time service status
